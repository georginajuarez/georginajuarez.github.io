A Bit About Me...

Hi! I’m Georgina.
I am Mexican born and Seattle raised. Like many other millennials, I’m a multi-hyphenate: An artist, teacher, and web developer who specializes in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I studied Interdiciplinary Art with an emphasis in photography (BA) at Seattle University, delving into visual arts, costume design, and photography.

I have spent the last few years working in education, all the while maintaining a side hustle of art projects to keep my creativity flowing. Recently, I've decided to expand my creative medium to include web development. I am currently working on a Certificate in Web Development at Seattle University. I would like to continue creating websites that are visually aesthetic and functional.
